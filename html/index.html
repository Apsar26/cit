Courses Offered

The Department of Computer Science is one of the largest and well established centres of learning computer science inSouth India offering B.E, B.Tech, M.E programmes.

B.E

Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science engineering is a full-time engineering undergraduate degree course in the field of computer science. It deals with the design, maintenance, construction and operation of computer hardware and software.

Btech

Bachelor of technology in computer science and engineering is a undergraduate degree which teaches a candidate about every aspect of Computer Engineering. It includes a detailed study of coding languages, along with various other important concepts on Computer Architecture, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, etc.

M.E

Master of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering is a knowledge oriented course
